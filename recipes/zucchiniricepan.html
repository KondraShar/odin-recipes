Zucchini Rice Pan

Description

Zucchini rice is a warm, comforting dish made by sautéing tender zucchini with garlic and onions until they soften and release their aroma, then folding in fluffy rice that absorbs all the fresh, savory flavors. The result is a light yet satisfying meal with a gentle sweetness from the zucchini and a cozy, homemade feel.

Legend has it that this meal was first improvised by a busy gardener named Lina, who returned home late one summer evening with nothing but leftover rice and a basket of zucchini. Too tired for anything fancy, she grated the zucchini straight into a pan, added the rice, and sprinkled in whatever herbs she had on hand. To her surprise, the dish tasted like the essence of her garden—simple, fresh, and unexpectedly comforting.

Ingredients

  • 1 zucchini
  • 125g rice
  • 150-200 g feta cheese
  • 750 ml vegetable stock
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp mediterranean herbs
  • thyme, oregano & dried basil
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the zucchini in half and slice. Peel the onion and garlic. Chop the onion finely.
  2. Cook the rice in the vegetable stock according to the package instructions. Then drain and reserve 150 ml of the stock. Heat the olive oil in a pan and sauté the zucchini briefly over high heat.
  3. Add the onions and sauté over medium heat until translucent. Press the garlic into the pan and sauté briefly. Add the onions and sauté over medium heat until translucent. Press in the garlic and sauté briefly. Then stir in the herbs and tomato paste and pour in 150 ml of broth.
  4. Add the cooked rice and half of the crumbled feta cheese and stir until melted. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
