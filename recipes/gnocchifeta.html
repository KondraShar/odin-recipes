Gnocchi Pan

Description

This gnocchi feta dish combines pillowy-soft gnocchi with tomatoes, olive oil, herbs, and a block of creamy feta baked until it melts into a rich, tangy sauce. The gnocchi absorb the flavors beautifully, creating a dish that’s both indulgent and bright, with the feta adding a savory depth that ties everything together.

Legend tells of a young street vendor in a Italian harbor town who invented the meal by accident. One busy market day, he was left with only a handful of gnocchi and a chunk of feta he forgot to sell. With customers still hungry, he tossed the ingredients with tomatoes and herbs, baked them quickly, and served the result. People were instantly hooked, praising the combination as bold, unexpected, and irresistibly comforting. From that day on, the vendor became famous for his “harbor gnocchi,” a dish that brought travelers back again and again.

Ingredients

  • 500 g gnocchi (from the refrigerated section
  • 150 g cherry tomatoes
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion (small)
  • 1 bunch basil
  • 100 ml plant-based cream
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 200 g chopped tomatoes (from a can)
  • 100 g feta cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Finely chop the onion and garlic. Wash the cherry tomatoes, bell pepper, and basil. Cut the cherry tomatoes in half, dice the bell pepper, and roughly chop the basil. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Sauté the onion and garlic for about 2 minutes. Add the gnocchi and continue to sauté for 3 minutes. Add the cherry tomatoes and bell pepper and continue to sauté for another 2 minutes. Add the tomato paste, chopped tomatoes, and plant-based cream. Stir to form a sauce. Add the cherry tomatoes and bell pepper for another 2 minutes.
  3. Add the tomato paste, chopped tomatoes, and plant-based cream, stir to form a sauce, and season with salt and pepper. Finally, add the basil to the pan, crumble the vegan feta, and serve the finished gnocchi pan with feta.
