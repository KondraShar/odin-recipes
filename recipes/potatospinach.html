Potato Spinach Casserole

Description

A potato spinach casserole is a hearty, oven-baked dish made by layering thinly sliced potatoes with fresh spinach, sautéed onions, and a creamy, herb-infused sauce. As it bakes, the potatoes become tender and golden at the edges while the spinach melts into the layers, creating a warm, comforting meal that’s both rustic and nourishing.

According to local kitchen lore, this casserole was first thrown together by a shepherd’s family living in a mountain cottage where winter supplies were scarce. One cold evening, with only a sack of potatoes, a bundle of late-season spinach, and a splash of cream left in the pantry, the family assembled everything into a single clay dish and slid it into the hearth. What emerged was a bubbling, fragrant casserole that kept them warm through the night. They shared the recipe with travelers, and soon it became a beloved comfort food throughout the region.

Ingredients

  • 800 g waxy potatoes, peeled and weighed
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 2 onions
  • 2-3 garlic cloves
  • 400 ml milk
  • 200 ml cream (30% fat)
  • 300 g frozen spinach
  • 100 g cheese of your choice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp marjoram
  • 1 tsp freshly ground pepper
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a pot and sauté onions for 2 minutes over medium heat until translucent.
  2. Add garlic and sauté for another 1-2 minutes.
  3. Stir in flour, gradually pour in milk and mix well. Add cream and bring to a boil briefly.
  4. Stir in the casserole seasoning and season with salt and pepper to taste. Feel free to season a little more generously, as the potatoes and spinach will absorb the flavor.
  5. Add the potatoes to the sauce and stir in gently. Cover and simmer on low heat for 10 minutes. The sauce will thicken due to the starch in the potatoes.
  6. Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 200 degrees Celsius (convection).
  7. After 10 minutes, stir in the spinach and mix well.
  8. Now pour the mixture into an ovenproof dish, sprinkle with cheese and bake for approx. 20-25 minutes.
